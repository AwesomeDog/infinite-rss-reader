The RSS reader you've been waiting for.

A beautiful, infinite-scrolling interface for your Thunderbird RSS feeds. Private, ultra-fast, and distraction-free. One single binary to rule them all.

Get Started View Source

Everything you need.

Designed for power users who care about privacy, performance, and aesthetics.

🚀

Infinite Scroll

Seamlessly browse hundreds of news items. Content pre-fetches and renders natively as you scroll without stuttering.

🔒

100% Private

Runs entirely on your machine. Zero cloud servers, zero tracking, and absolutely no data collection.

📦

Zero Dependencies

A single Go binary containing everything. No npm, no Docker, no heavy runtimes required to get started.

🔄

Two-Way Sync

Read an article in the browser, and it instantly marks as read in Thunderbird. Everything stays perfectly synchronized.

📱

Mobile Ready

Fluid responsive design out of the box. Easily access your feeds from any mobile device on your local network.

🎨

Adaptive Themes

Automatically detects your system's preferences. Smoothly toggle between beautifully crafted dark and light modes.
